Era IV

From the game's manual:

"A fearsome warrior, Barnak is extremely violent. His enemies are forced to admit that he handles his weapons and magical powers with panache, power and majesty.

Barnak is a mean fighter, an unbeatable magician and a terrifying orator. He can hurt as much with his vicious tongue as he can by his weapons.

Before him, even the bravest challengers must admit defeat.

Always accompanied by Voydh, his ebony black dragon, Barnak drapes his blackness over others, as though his shadow bespeaks his very essence.

The scarlet light that flashes from his eyes sows doubt amongst his adversaries as to his true nature."

He tried to usurp the throne of Taskan IV at the end of the Civil War. When Hype tried to defend the king, Barnak turned him into stone and sent him 200 years into the past.

During the game you will meet Barnak only twice: at the very beginning, when Gogoud tells the story of how Hype's statue ended up in his Manor, and at the end, for the final boss fight.
