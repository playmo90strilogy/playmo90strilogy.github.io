Did you know that Alex needs calcium which you find in milk which comes from cows to stay strong and healthy? Here is a great idea and it tastes good too!!

Ingredients
You will need:
2 scoops of vanilla ice cream
1/2 cup of frozen fruit (Rasperies, strawberries and peaches are all good ideas!)
1 egg
2 cups of milk



Method
What to do:

In a food processor or blender mix the ice cream, fruits, egg and milk together.

Alex says:
The result is a yummy ice cream shake!!!
