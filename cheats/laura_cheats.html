Here are the cheat codes for the PC game of Laura's Happy Adventures.

To use the composed-cheats (the ones that have to be typed after another cheat) :

  • 1. type GIRLPOWER everytime you change scenary to activate cheats mode.
  • 2. Type the first cheat (C1, C2, C3, etc...) and a box will appear with the explenation of the cheat.
  • 3. Wait a few second, until the background music becomes a bit quiter, then press Enter.
  • 4. Now Laura will be stuck until you press Enter again. In this timeframe where Laura is stuck, you have to type the second cheat (1, 2, etc...) and then press Enter.

For the other cheats (not composed) simply type them during gameplay.

You'll notice that some of the cheats don't have a description (???). That's because we couldn't figure out what they do yet.
If you find out the function of a cheat, you can let me know by sending an email at playmo90strilogy@gmail.com, so I can update the table!😄
