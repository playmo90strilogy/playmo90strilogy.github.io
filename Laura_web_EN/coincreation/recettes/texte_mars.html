I love chocolate chips and peanut butter...
How can we go wrong with these cookies?



You will need:

1/2 cup of butter
1 cup of white sugar
1 cup of crunchy peanut butter
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup chopped peanuts
1 cup of chocolate chips



What to do:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl cream together the butter and sugar.
Add all the peanut butter and cream together.
Beat in the egg and the vanilla.
Combine the flour, baking soda and the baking powder and mix into the creamed mixture.
Stir in the peanuts and the chocolate chips until you have a crumbly mixture.
Shape into 1/2 inch balls on a greased baking tray.
Place in the oven for approx. 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
Remove from the oven and allow to cool for several minutes.
Makes about 3 dozen cookies!
 Enjoy!
