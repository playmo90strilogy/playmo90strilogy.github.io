Brigands

Era I

In this Era the Brigands are your enemies. You meet them in the Forest, where they claim a payment for your passage. They are, therefore, only thieves seeking for gold and weapons.

They will attack you by throwing knives and axes at you, or with arch and arrows.


  • Name: Malio. He's the first Brigand you will meet. He will try to steal your Plastyks and weapons in the Forest.

Era II

In Era II, Hype and the Brigands will become allies thanks to Karon, their leader, that is seeking revenge for his father Bolduk, architect of the Field of Courage.

Taskan I never paid Bolduk for his work, that's why Karon wants your help to get into the Field of Courage's Treasure room. As a reward for your help she will give you the Brigands' Insignia, symbol of your alliance.

You will find the Brigands in their Village, and in the Forest where, before your alliance, they will attack you with knives, axes, crossbow and arch and arrows.


  • Name: Terryo. He's Karon's husband and Nolhin's father. You can find him at the Brigands' Village after your quest in the Field of Courage.
  • Name: Turkoth. He is the village's forger and will provide you the fake-signed pass that you need to enter into the Field of Courage.
  • Name: Mordor. He's the first Brigand you meet in the Village. He's suspicious of Hype and doesn't trust him until he has seen that Karon trusts him as well. Then he even sharpens Hype's bolts.
  • Name: Pratt. When you meet Karon for the first time in the Forest, by her order, he takes your weapons and Plastyks.
  • Name: Hennaff. He can be found in the Forest and infroms you that Karon went to the Brigands' Village.

Era III

Nolhin, the new Brigands' leader, is the daughter of Karon and Terryo, and so the grandaughter of Bolduk.

She'll inform you that now the Brigands work for you. She will also tell you that king Taskan II talked to the Brigands and gave them a map showing the existence of a Lost City. They will help you find its entrance.

You will find the Brigands in the Forest and at the Brigands' Village.


  • Name: Exar. He tells you that he's happy the Brigands have adopted your noble cause because, with the Civil War and the lack of treasures and gold, the Brigands' trade was on the verge of dying out. He can be found in the mess hall of the Village and warns Hype about the Ghosts in the Forest.
  • Name: Galnoth. He can be found in the mess hall of the Village. He tells Hype that now the Brigands have very close links with Zatila and that he's worried about the dragon because he's been very distant lately.
  • Name: Anoth. He is a Hype fan, and admires him since he was a kid when he learned to know him by listening to the stories Karon and Terryo told about him. He can be found as soon as you enter in the Brigands' Village and gives Hype some bolts that he has made specifically for him.
  • Name: Havarnal. He can be found in the Forest looking for the dolmen that will grant you access to the Lost City. He will also tell you about why Taskn II gave them the map to find it.

Era IV

The Brigands are now part of the Resistance lead by Vibe, against the reign of Barnak.

The dark knight Guards kicked them out of their Village where only Nolhin, now an old woman, remains because considered harmless and amusing by the dark knight Guards.

You will find the Brigands in the Town of Torras, at the Inn, waiting for you with Vibe.


  • Name: Bygras. He's part of the resistance and can be found at the Inn with Vibe.
  • Name: Rivesth. He's part of the resistance and can be found at the Inn with Vibe.
