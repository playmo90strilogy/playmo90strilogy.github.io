Alex's mom loves chocolate chips cookies and wants to share her recipe with you!

Ingredients
To make chocolate chips cookies you'll need:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 cup butter
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup of flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2/3 cup of chocolate chips
1/2 cup of chopped walnuts



Method
To make chocolate chips cookies:

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
Beat the white sugar, the brown sugar and the butter together.
Add the beaten egg and mix well.
Stir in the flour and the baking soda.
Add the chocolate chips and nuts.
Grease several baking trays and spoon the cookie dough in small well spaced balls.
Place the trays in the oven and bake 8 - 9 minutes or until the cookies are golden brown
Remove from the oven and allow to cool for several minutes.
Place the cookies in an air tight container to enjoy now or later!
