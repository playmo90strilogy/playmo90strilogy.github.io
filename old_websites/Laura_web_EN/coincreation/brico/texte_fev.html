Make your very personnal Hair Combs and show off to your friends!

How can you make your very own individual
Hair Combs?
It's easy, you just need a bit of imagination!




You'll need:

One or several fairly large, flat hair combs.

Some brightly colored buttons or sequins.

Strong glue.

 


What to do?


For a single slide, the buttons or sequins should be the same. Choose the color or colors that you like best.

Put a blob of glue on the back of each button or sequin and stick them side by side along the length of your hair comb.

And that is all there is to it! You've made your very own individual hair comb. Now you can have fun creating some more!
