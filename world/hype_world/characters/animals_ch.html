Animals

Era I

Wolves: only found at the beginning of the Forest in Era I. They will run to you and attack. Easily defeatable by hitting them with the sword.

Bats: found in the Forest and in the sewers under the Town of Torras. They will fly to you and attack.
You can defeat them by afar using the crossbow, or you can wait for them to come to you and hit them with the sword once they're close enough.

Boars: found in the Forest, like wolves, boars will run to you and attack. Easily defeatable by hitting them with the sword.

Era II

Wolves: Not present.

Bats: found at Gogoud's Manor, infesting the suspended bridge, and in the Forest.

Boars: Not present.

Era III

Wolves: Not present.

Bats: found in the Field of Courage, the Forest, and at the top of the bell tower in the Monastery.

Boars: found in the Forest.

Era IV

Wolves: Not present.

Bats: found in the Black Tower, the Dungeons, and at Gogoud's Manor.

Boars: Not present.
