Citizen

Era I

He's a citizen of the Town of Torras.


  • Name: Dandurand. He can be found at the Inn. He tells you about the tension between the crown and the Monastery. He can't stand neither Taskan I nor the Monastery because of their endless thrist for power.
  • Name: Lanctot. He's on the street outside the Inn. He warns you about Granslak, and gives you a hint on how to defeat him.

Era II

  • Name: Belhair. He can be found in the streets of Torras. He works for Rajoth at the Laboratory and he claims to have strange visions now. He also tells you that the magicians are being held prisoners.
  • Name: Belanger. He can be found in the streets of Torras. He works for Rajoth as well and recognizes Hype from the picture on the Quaesitus. He knows Hype is looking for Gogoud and asks him for 20 Plastyks in exchange for informations about the magician.
  • Name: Thoutant. He can be found at the Inn. He misses the old days when the crown was only at war with the Monastery. He doesn't agree with the innovations brought by Taskan II and is nostalgic of Taskan I's reign. He's also worried about the men of the Field of Courage and their possible rebellion against the king.

Era III

  • Name: Boudroh. He can be found at the entrance of Torras. He's frightened and tired of the Civil War and tells you about the secret passages on the roofs of the town.
  • Name: Laffyth. He can be found at the Inn. He's the husband of Kirana and he's tired of the Civil War because he can't enjoy himself with his friends anymore. He tells Hype that he shoud leave the town because people have no faith in the king since the legend of the Black Knight has spread. He's also afraid that the men in the Field of Courage are hatching a plan against Taskan III.

Era IV

  • Name: Bilodo. He can be found in the streets of Torras. He thinks Hype is there to test his loyalty to Barnak and recites him the pledge.
  • Name: Paquin. He can be found at the Inn. He's surprised to see a foreign knight and tries to test your strenght by calling the Black Knight's Guards. Fortunately Galarno Jr. stops him in time and makes him understand who Hype is.
