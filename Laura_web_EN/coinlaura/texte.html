Laura has gone off to explore the attic! She has found a diamond in an old trunk. But this is no ordinary diamond, it's a magic diamond!

What happens next? She must make all five faces of the diamond light up by making her friends and family happy! Using the keyboard, you can decide what will happen to Laura as she sets out on an exciting adventure into the heart of the peaceful Playmobil village.

Run through the narrow streets , and along the paths which lead to the mine and you'll find lots of small adventures to keep you busy - a grandfather who claims to have met fairies, a little brother who's lost Dad's binoculars and much more!

From the attic to the cellar, from the garden to the mysterious fairy world - you're leading the way! Search for secret messages, special objects or hidden passages, and meet the people who live in this mysterious town! The local shopkeeper, the tramp and the acrobat may be keeping some strange secrets - it's up to you to ask them questions and discover these valuable clues!
Still not sure where to begin? Perhaps Clara, the Fortune Teller holds the key to the mystery? Why don't you go and ask her?
Back home the adventure isn't over: new tasks still await you! Between the cook who needs nuts to bake a cake and Mom who wants you to look after your little sister Elise, you won't have time to get bored!

 

But maybe you want to play your own game? Well, you can redecorate all the rooms in the house in your own way with the help of your magic wand!
